Practical Information

Venue

CLIN 34 will take place on August 30, 2024 in the Lipsius building of Leiden University, in the heart of Leiden, around a 15 minute walk from the central station.
Address
Cleveringaplaats 1, Leiden

Leiden Travelling Information
Directions from Leiden Centraal Train Station

Talk Instructions

In our morning and afternoon talk sessions, in order to adhere to time constraints, please allot at most 15 minutes to your respective talk. The remaining 5 minutes can be used for questions from the audience.

Poster Instructions

The poster boards at the conference measure 1m x 1.25m and are designed to accommodate posters in portrait mode up to A0 format. If you prefer to present your poster in landscape mode, please ensure it is limited to A1 format.
