CLIN 34 — Leiden 2024

The 34th Meeting of Computational Linguistics in The Netherlands (CLIN 34), organized by the Leiden University Centre for Linguistics (LUCL), in collaboration with the Leiden Institute of Advanced Computer Science (LIACS), and the Leiden University Centre for Digital Humanities (LUCDH), Leiden University, is set to be held on August 30, 2024 at the Science Park campus of Leiden University, Leiden, The Netherlands.
